Code of Conduct

  • Be Respectful

    Respect the opinions and contributions of others within the community.

  • Avoid Offensive Content

    Do not post content that may be considered offensive or harmful to others.

  • Avoid Derogatory Language

    Avoid using derogatory or disrespectful language when communicating with others.

  • No Personal Attacks

    Do not engage in personal attacks or harassment against any community member.

  • Respect Intellectual Property

    Respect intellectual property rights and avoid plagiarism or copyright infringement.

  • Account Bans

    Non-compliance with the guidelines may result in bans.
